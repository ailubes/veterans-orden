# Prompt for Illustrated Explainer — Мережа Вільних Людей

## Project Overview

Create an illustrated infographic/explainer in **Ukrainian** for new members of "Мережа Вільних Людей" (Network of Free People) — a Ukrainian civic political action network with the mission to unite 1,000,000 citizens to influence political processes democratically.

**Tagline:** "ГУРТУЄМОСЬ, ЩОБ ВПЛИВАТИ!" (We unite to influence!)

---

## Brand Assets Reference

### Logo
The organization logo is available at: `/public/logo-freepeople.svg`

**Logo Description:** A circular badge featuring two hands reaching toward each other with Ukrainian text "МЕРЕЖА ВІЛЬНИХ ЛЮДЕЙ" around the perimeter and "ГО" (meaning "Громадська Організація" — Public Organization) at the bottom. The hands symbolize unity, cooperation, and collective action.

### Color Palette (Timber Design System)
- **Canvas (Background):** `#f4f1eb` — warm cream/parchment
- **Timber Dark (Primary Text):** `#2c2824` — dark brown
- **Timber Medium:** `#5c5551` — medium brown
- **Accent:** `#d45d3a` — warm red-orange (for CTAs)
- **Success Green:** `#22c55e`
- **Role Colors:**
  - Gray: `#9ca3af` (Supporter)
  - Blue: `#3b82f6` (Candidate)
  - Green: `#22c55e` (Member)

  - Red: `#ef4444` (Regional)
  - Gold: `#eab308` (National)
  - Platinum: `#e5e7eb` with gold accents (Guide)

### Typography
- **Headlines:** Syne (bold, geometric)
- **Body:** Space Mono or system sans-serif
- **Style:** Constructivist/brutalist Ukrainian aesthetic

## Design Guidelines

### Overall Style
- **Ukrainian constructivist/brutalist aesthetic** — geometric shapes, bold lines
- **Warm, approachable** — not corporate, feels grassroots
- **Clear hierarchy** — easy to scan
- **Mobile-friendly** — works on phone screens
- **Print-friendly** — can be printed as poster/flyer

### Visual Elements
- Use the organization logo prominently
- Include role badge icons from `/public/images/role-badges/`
- Ukrainian flag colors (blue/yellow) as accents where appropriate
- Hands/unity imagery
- Network/connection metaphors (nodes, lines, web)
- Growth imagery (trees, mountains, stairs)

### Typography Rules
- All text in Ukrainian
- Clear, readable fonts
- Adequate contrast for accessibility
- Bold for headings, regular for body

### Layout
- Vertical scroll format (for web/mobile)
- OR multi-panel horizontal (for presentation/print)
- Section dividers with visual interest
- White space for breathing room
